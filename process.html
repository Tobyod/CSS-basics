Process

General Information/Advice

The best way to improve is probably to practice, and to not be afraid to use references when you do not know how to draw something. When drawing I personally like to use ibisPaint and Procreate when creating digital art, and a mechanical pencil and sketchbook when creating traditional art.

Sketching

When drawing a scene I usually start by sketching the main focus of the drawing e.g. the background or the character followed by any other secondary details. Keep in mind a sketch is just a rough version of a drawing, so its fine if it isnt perfect.

Main Colours and Cleanup

After the initial sketch you should decide on the main colours/hue of the art, so if you want it to be day time night time, and what colours you generally wanna use, next you clean up the sketch (basically just erasing extra lines and redrawing parts to look better if needed)

Basic shading

Next you should add basic shading. Its important to start big and focus on finer details later. I recommend using references and shapes over lines when shading, also be sure to put more detail into parts of the drawing you want to be perceived as closer to add depth to your drawings, and to draw your eye to important parts of your drawing.

Finishing Touches

Basically for this final step I first, add any extra lighting/shading details I need, usually to add more contrast, then I just go over and add anything else I wanna add e.g. adding background elements, adding extra hair detail, eye detail etc this is the part where I focus in on stuff since the big picture stuff is locked in.
